Dark Noose Defined by its lust for power, Noose is a huge coalition of mercenaries that decided to claim the world as their own.

White Moon Defined by its silence, Moon appeared after Noose used its first superweapons, but its armies won't say a word.

Red Gem Defined by its belief in mystical power, Gem strives to destroy Noose as revenge for past atrocities.

Orange Sun Defined by its warrior spirit, Sun sought out combat and increasingly needed mercenaries to fight their battles.

Yellow Coin Defined by its fiscal wizardry, Coin split off from Noose after deciding that seizing the globe by force was ill-advised.

Green Leaf Defined by its ties to the land, Leaf has significant natural resources and fights a defensive war against Noose.

Blue Wave Defined by its proud history, Wave was devastated by a superweapon and wants lands Noose raised from the sea.

Purple Book Defined by its technical mastery, Book seeks to take Noose's advanced weaponry and use it for constructive purposes.
